Frequently Asked Questions

What does it mean that $CATBOMB is a gold-backed token? +

Being gold-backed means that a significant portion of CATBOMB's revenue (40%) is used to purchase real gold (XAUt - Tether Gold), which serves as a reserve supporting the token's value. This doesn't imply that each token can be directly redeemed for gold, but rather that the project's ecosystem holds gold assets to bolster confidence, reduce volatility, and provide tangible backing for $CATBOMB. In essence, it adds an extra layer of stability and trust for holders, knowing that real-world assets partially support the token.

How does CATBOMB ensure transparency and trust in its ecosystem? +

CATBOMB maintains transparency by recording all key funds and processes on the blockchain. The prize pool wallet, charity wallet, and burn wallet are all public addresses that the community can view in real time. Likewise, information about gold reserve purchases (the 40% allocation) will be shared through regular reports or an online dashboard, so everyone can see how much gold has been accumulated. The smart contracts governing the prize distribution and token economics are open for audit, meaning the community or independent auditors can review the code to ensure everything operates as intended.

How can we be sure that you actually donate the money after deducting expenses? +

CATBOMB has implemented several measures to ensure transparency and credibility:

  • Smart Contracts: Donations are controlled by transparent smart contracts, which can be verified on the blockchain.
  • Transparent Dashboard: We have a dashboard that displays the charity wallet balance and donations in real-time.
  • Third-Party Audits: We may use third-party audit services to verify donations and the correct use of funds.
  • Disclosure of Transfer Proof: Every donation will be accompanied by publicly disclosed transfer proof that can be verified.
  • Quarterly Reports: We will produce quarterly reports on donations and fund usage to keep the community continuously informed.
Why did CATBOMB choose TON Blockchain? +

We chose TON due to its exclusive strategic partnership with Telegram, which boasts 900 million monthly active users. This partnership provides seamless integration with interconnected wallets and facilitates easy interaction between platforms. TON's scalability, rapid community expansion, and strong foundation system align perfectly with our vision of transitioning users to Web 3.0 while maintaining user-friendly accessibility.
